Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AEO Tracker ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to create and secure your account.
  • Search data: keywords, domains, competitor domains, and cities you enter into our tools, along with the generated reports.
  • Usage data: basic technical information such as browser type and general activity within the app, used to improve reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the AI visibility, sentiment, ranking, local, and citation analysis features.
  • To save your search history so you can revisit past reports.
  • To maintain account security and authenticate your login.
  • To improve the reliability and accuracy of our tools.

3. Third-Party Services

To generate reports, AEO Tracker sends your search queries to the following third-party services:

  • Google Search data (via Serper.dev) — to retrieve real-time search results.
  • Groq AI — to analyze search results and generate insights.
  • Supabase — to securely store your account and search history.

These providers process data only to deliver the requested functionality and are bound by their own privacy and security practices.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and search history are stored securely using Supabase, with row-level security ensuring only you can access your own data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights

  • You can view and delete your search history at any time from within the app.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us.
  • You can log out at any time, which ends your active session.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to keep you logged in between visits. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of AEO Tracker after changes means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the contact details provided on our website.
