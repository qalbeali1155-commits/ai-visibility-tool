AEO Basics
What Is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Why It Matters in 2026
6 min read  ·  Updated July 2026
Traditional SEO focused on ranking in Google's blue links. AEO is the new discipline focused on getting your brand mentioned inside AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.

Search behavior is shifting. Instead of typing a keyword and scrolling through ten blue links, people are asking AI assistants direct questions and trusting the answer they get back. This shift means visibility inside an AI-generated answer is becoming as valuable as ranking #1 on Google once was.

How AEO differs from traditional SEO

Traditional SEO optimizes for crawlers and ranking algorithms. AEO optimizes for how large language models synthesize an answer from multiple sources. That means clarity, authority, and being cited by trusted sources matter more than keyword density.

Why this matters for your business

  • AI answers often skip listing multiple options — being the one brand mentioned carries outsized value.
  • Users increasingly trust AI summaries over clicking through multiple websites.
  • Early movers in AEO are building a visibility advantage before the space becomes competitive.

Tools like AEO Tracker help you measure where you currently stand — whether AI engines mention your brand today, and what competitors are being recommended instead.

Brand Strategy
Why Your Brand Might Not Be Mentioned by ChatGPT
5 min read
The most common reasons brands get skipped by AI search engines — and the practical fixes that help.

Common reasons brands are missing from AI answers

  • Low domain authority: AI models favor sources frequently cited elsewhere on the web.
  • Thin or outdated content: if your site doesn't clearly answer the question, models skip it.
  • Missing structured information: clear headings and FAQs help AI extract and cite your content.
  • Weak local signals: inconsistent NAP details hurt local AI visibility.

Practical fixes

Start by identifying exactly where you stand — run a visibility check on your target keywords to see who AI currently recommends instead of you. Prioritize the single highest-impact fix before spreading effort across many small changes.

Local SEO
Local AI Visibility: Winning "Near Me" Searches
4 min read
What actually influences whether an AI assistant recommends your business for local queries.

When someone asks an AI assistant "best plumber near me," the model draws on a mix of local business listings, review signals, and general search data to form an answer.

Key factors for local AI visibility

  • Consistent business name, address, and phone number across your site and directories.
  • A healthy volume of genuine, recent customer reviews.
  • Clear service-area pages mentioning the specific cities you serve.
  • Being mentioned alongside competitors in local roundup articles.

Benchmarking yourself against nearby competitors for your core service keywords is the fastest way to see where the gaps are.

Trust Signals
Citations Matter More Than Ever
5 min read
How citation authority works and why it's becoming a core AI ranking signal.

Just as backlinks were a core trust signal for traditional search engines, citations — being referenced by other reputable sources — are becoming a core trust signal for AI systems.

How to build citation authority

  • Get featured in industry roundups, comparison articles, and review sites relevant to your niche.
  • Publish original data, research, or expert commentary other sites want to reference.
  • Maintain an accurate, detailed presence on major directories relevant to your industry.

Tracking your citation score over time helps you understand whether your authority-building efforts are translating into AI visibility.
