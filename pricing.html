Frequently Asked Questions
Do Free searches reset daily?
No — the Free plan gives you 5 searches total, one time, tied to your email. Once used, you'll need to upgrade to Client or Paid to keep scanning.
How do I get Client access?
Client access is reserved for businesses we personally onboard — it's not a public self-signup plan. Reach out to us and we'll set it up for you directly.
How do I upgrade to Paid?
Sign up for a free account first, then contact us to move to the Paid plan. Your plan is activated on our end and takes effect immediately.
What counts as one "search"?
Each report you generate — AI Visibility, Brand Sentiment, Why Not Ranking, Local AI, Citation Score, or a Geo-Grid scan — counts as one search against your plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, there's no lock-in contract. Message us anytime to downgrade or cancel your plan.
